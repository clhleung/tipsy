About Us

Ever had a few drinks and heard someone say "Aw I'm not that drunk, I can totally drive"?

Tipsy Trials is a service people can use to see just how alcohol has really affected them.

How do we do this? Here you can test your drunk self with a series of games and see for yourself!

All you need to do is sign up and set a sober baseline score, then you can test yourself whenever you like.
