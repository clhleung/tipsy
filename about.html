How to Play

The pictures below give an example of each game.

Look at each one before beginning your trials!


You'll play four games in total.

Play as fast as you can!

Sentence Matching

Type the sentence that appears exactly as shown. Write 3 sentences correctly to continue

Simple Math

Complete 5 math problems using either the keyboard or numberpad

Color Matching

You will be given a Color to match using the buttons below

Be sure to click the color it says and not color it's displayed in!

Maze Game

The goal is to move the black circle to the green one

Use the directional keys on your keyboard to do this as quickly as you can!
